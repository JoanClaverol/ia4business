      I have been working at Impala Studios full-time for more than a year

      Pros

      Everyone is working in their field to make the best product (technology, product, UI, UX) and ambitious to achieve this. Management pressure does not exist here, there is plenty of ambition for everyone. Fun to work with top notch pinball machines, table football and music. No-nonsense atmosphere (it is so much more fun working at Impala than at a large corporate company with all those 'politics' and 'networks')

      Negatives

      - fixed working hours (but work is done here)
